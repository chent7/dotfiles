### HOW TO DEPLOY ###
1. Grab spectrwm.conf and keybinds.conf and place in ~/.config/spectrwm/ or PATHS WHERE SPECTRWM WILL SEARCH FOR CONFIGS (see man page)
2. Make sure fonts and packages/programs in spectrwm.conf are installed
3. Install spectrwm and launch from X

PS. Make sure you have your own autostart (xinit/xprofile) or modify spectrwm.conf (see man page), as well as configs for rofi...etc
